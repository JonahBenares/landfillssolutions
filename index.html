About

Learn More About Us


Landfills EnviroTech Inc is a subsidiary of Energreen Power Inter-Island Corp. that is dedicated to providing cost-effective solutions for the continuing problems of mounting municipal wastes and landfills of many cities and municipalities in the Philippines.


What We Do


Waste to energy is one of the most challenging projects to try to develop, approve, finance, and implement in the Philippines. It is not made easier by the local elections every three (3) years. In Landfills EnviroTech we combine the group’s proven expertise in the essential disciplines needed to make WTE projects happen. Project development, locations assessments, competitive and clean power, logistics management, LGU approvals, regulatory and environmental compliances, technology matching, strategic alliances, project finance, risk mitigation, and operations management. We aim to eliminate the need to close overflowing dumpsites and avoid opening new ones that damage more neighborhoods and environment. We provide end-to-end solutions from waste to energy.


Focus

Landfills EnviroTech concentrates on solutions for the island LGU’s with waste volumes of 200 to 500MT per day. In energy, 5 to 10mw of power generation.



Benefits

LGU Benefits

Landfills EnviroTech start from landfills and complement the existing infrastructure and contracts in garbage collection, hauling, and tipping. We seek to continue the process of the responsible and economical disposal of waste that are already in the landfills. Typically, the LGU as a benefit can supply part of its own electricity requirement from the waste to energy project.
